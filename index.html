Our Mission

We believe access to nutritious food is a human right, not a privilege.

Founded in 2021, Neighbor's Pantry has served over 15,000 families by redistributing surplus food from local farms, bakeries, and grocery stores. We operate with zero waste and 100% transparency.

Impact so far: 250,000 meals served, 45 community partners.
